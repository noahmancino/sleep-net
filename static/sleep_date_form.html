Welcome to Sleep Tracker!

This tool helps you identify how different factors affect your sleep quality. Simply add questions about your daily activities that you'll answer with a yes or no each morning. Over time, we'll analyze the data to show you insights into how these activities impact your sleep. Try to limit yourself to only a few of the most important relationships, as this makes the statistical relationships easier to analyze and interpret

Examples of questions you might add:

  • Did I drink coffee at night?
  • Did I exercise in the evening?
